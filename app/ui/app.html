How settlement is proven
At game finalised, the keeper fetches a Merkle proof of the final score from TxLine and submits it to the program. The program CPIs into the txoracle to verify it against the on-chain daily root — a wrong score is rejected by math, then payouts follow the Trepa median-gate engine you see ticking above.

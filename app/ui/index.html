How it works
1

Pick a score, stake USDC

Choose the exact full-time score you believe in — 2–1, 0–0, anything — and back it with a USDC stake before kick-off locks the market.

2

Watch your live PnL

Goals move the whole leaderboard in real time. See exactly what your stake would pay if the match ended right now — like a ticking stock position.

3

Closest guesses get paid

At full-time the final score is proven on-chain. Beat the median distance and you split the losers' pool, weighted by how close you were.

🛡️

No referee to bribe. Settlement runs against TxLine's Merkle-proven score feed — the program verifies the final score cryptographically before a single cent moves. A wrong score is mathematically rejected, not just "against the rules." We proved it live: a tampered 3–1 proof fails on-chain, the real 2–1 passes.
